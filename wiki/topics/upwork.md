# Upwork

## Summary

Upwork became an early traction channel for Adam's AI automation work, moving from outbound proposal practice into first replies, a first paid workflow-translation gig, and the beginning of repeat client relationships.

## Key Points

- Early traction came after repeated proposal volume rather than immediate wins.
- The first paid work centered on translating Zapier workflows into `n8n`.
- The journal suggests a deliberate discipline around submitting proposals consistently.
- By late July 2025, Upwork had shifted from experimentation into a live client channel.
