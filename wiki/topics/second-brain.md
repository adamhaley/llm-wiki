# Second Brain

## Summary

A practical second-brain workflow keeps capture cheap, preserves raw material, and promotes only the ideas that become durable enough to deserve canonical wiki pages.

## Key Points

- Capture should stay cheap.
- Promotion should stay selective.
- Deterministic maintenance and semantic meaning-making should remain separate.
- Scheduled sync is helpful during idle clean periods and should safely skip during active work.
